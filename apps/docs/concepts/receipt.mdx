---
title: Receipt
description: Hash-chained JSONL feed of every gated call.
---

Every gated call yields a `ReceiptV1`:

- **`kind`** — `spend` (buyer-initiated) or `earn` (seller-initiated).
- **`nonce`** — gapless, per-agent, monotonically increasing from 0.
- **`prev_receipt_hash`** — `null` at genesis, otherwise the previous receipt's hash.
- **`receipt_hash`** — `sha256(canonicalJson(receipt without receipt_hash))`.

Verify any feed with `@leash/core` `verifyReceiptChain` or the `leash-conformance` CLI shipped from `@leash/testing`.
